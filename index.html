Climate Change is important!

Introduction to Climate Change

Climate change is a dramatic or subtle general change in climate due to humans, sudden changes in the environment, or on its own.

For example, we are in a 'warm' ice age. However, several millennia ago, we were in a very cold ice age. However, starting from a few million years ago, the Earth has warmed up on its own.

However, humans have had a HUGE impact on our environment and the Earth's climate.
